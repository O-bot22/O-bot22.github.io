Hearing from locals

Within the project, our team recorded anonymous street surveys with residents of Puerto Real. The survey included a range of economic, demographic, and housing-related factors. This data would be used to map and analyze energy poverty in the area. Throughout the surveys we would find that there was lower participation than expected due to many different factors, but the most prominent being the lack of trust. Overall, we collected surveys from 27 residents of Puerto Real and aggregated the data across all of Puerto Real to analyze what we were given.
