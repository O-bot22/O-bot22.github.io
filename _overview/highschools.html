Teaching Energy Efficiency

One of the key components of our project was workshops with high school students. These workshops consisted of a presentation given by our team about energy efficiency. In total, we presented seven different groups, with students ages ranging from 14-18. The presentation covered what energy is, how energy is used, how to be more energy efficient, and activities to engage the students in the topics. Along with this the students completed anonymous surveys before and after to access how much they learned.
