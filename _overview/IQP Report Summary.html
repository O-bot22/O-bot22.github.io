Final Project Report

Energy poverty (EP), the inability of a household to maintain a socially and materially required level of energy services, presents a significant challenge for residents of Puerto Real, Spain. Our team worked with Professor Laura Romero and Pilar Monsalvete of the University of Cádiz (UCA) to work on spreading awareness about EP and mapping it in Puerto Real. For this project, surveys and interviews were conducted, workshops were held, and a website was created to summarize our findings.
