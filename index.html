Project Overview

Welcome to the WPI IQP project on energy poverty (sponsored by Universidad de Cádiz, UCA).

Our research explores energy access,sustainability, poverty through interactive mapping and analysis.

About

This is an Interdisciplinary Qualifying Project (IQP) at WPI focused on energy poverty, equity, and data visualization.
